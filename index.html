How PDF Processing Works

When you upload a PDF for summarizing or asking questions:

1. Temporary Processing (Node.js Backend)

  • Your device sends the PDF content securely to our Node.js server, which is hosted on Render (a third-party cloud infrastructure provider).
  • The backend does not store, save the PDF or extracted text.
  • The server only forwards the text to the Gemini API and sends the result back to your device.
  • All data is immediately discarded after processing.

2. Processing by Google Gemini API (Third-Party Processor)

The PDF content is sent securely to Google's Gemini API for processing to generate summaries and answers. During this process, your data leaves our control and is subject to Google's policies.

CRITICAL NOTICE: Data Used for Model Training

As a new application, we currently use the Free Tier of the Google Gemini API.

According to Google's policy for free services, your uploaded PDF content and prompts (input data) may be used by Google to train and improve their foundational AI models.

DO NOT upload any confidential or proprietary documents. We recommend against uploading any sensitive data while we operate on the Free Tier.
