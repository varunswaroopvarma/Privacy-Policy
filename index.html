How PDF Processing Works

When you upload a PDF for summarizing or asking questions:

1. Temporary Processing (Node.js Backend)

  • Your device sends the PDF content securely to our Node.js server.
  • The backend does not store, save, or log the PDF or extracted text.
  • The server only forwards the text to the Gemini API and sends the result back to your device.
  • All data is immediately discarded after processing.

2. Processing by Google Gemini API

The text is temporarily processed by Google's Gemini API to generate summaries or answers.

Important: According to Google’s policy, data sent through Gemini API is not used to train or improve their foundational AI models.

For free usage of Googles's Gemini API, Google may use PDF's or data sent by the user in the app to train or improve their foundational AI models. To learn more refer to Google privacy policy or Google Gemini API privacy policy.
